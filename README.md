# 🐄 Automatic Cattle Feeding Machine

An automated electromechanical system designed to improve livestock feeding efficiency by dispensing predetermined quantities of feed at scheduled intervals. The project minimizes manual labor, reduces feed wastage, and promotes consistent feeding schedules using a microcontroller-based control system.

---

## 📖 Overview

The **Automatic Cattle Feeding Machine** is a smart farming solution developed to automate the feeding process in dairy and livestock farms. Traditional feeding methods are labor-intensive and often lead to inconsistent feed distribution, overfeeding, and wastage.

This project integrates mechanical design, embedded systems, and automation to deliver accurate amounts of cattle feed automatically using a motor-driven dispensing mechanism controlled by a programmable microcontroller.

---

## ⚙️ Working Principle

1. Feed is loaded into the storage hopper.
2. The feeding schedule is configured through the microcontroller.
3. At the programmed time, the controller activates the motor.
4. The motor drives the screw conveyor (auger) to transport feed.
5. Feed is dispensed into the feeding tray.
6. Sensors monitor the feed quantity and hopper level.
7. Once the required quantity is dispensed, the motor automatically stops.
8. The process repeats according to the programmed schedule.
